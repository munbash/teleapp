Supporting Diabetes Management in Rural Communities

About TeleApp

TeleApp is a simple web application built to help nurses and healthcare workers manage and support diabetic patients in underserved rural areas. This tool offers basic health information, promotes diabetes education, and improves communication between caregivers and patients.

Patient Check-In

Use this section to log patient updates, monitor blood sugar levels, and provide health tips. (Future updates will include forms and remote reporting.)

Diabetes Tips

  • Eat a balanced diet with more vegetables and whole grains
  • Stay active even light walking can help
  • Monitor blood sugar regularly
  • Take medications as prescribed
  • Stay hydrated and reduce sugary drinks
